Challenge

Alex launched ChatGPT Central to fill a clear gap: busy professionals were hungry for smart, high-quality AI insights they could trust and act on. While the publication quickly gained traction and built a loyal readership, monetization posed a significant hurdle.

Committed to delivering real value to their audience, they knew it was time to try something new. After all, revenue was a priority, but audience trust and experience came first.

A smarter, more aligned solution was needed.
